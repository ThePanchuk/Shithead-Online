Special cards ▾
  • 2 — Wild · always playable
  • 3 — Mirror · plays as the card below it
  • 7 — Next must play < 7
  • 8 — Skip the next player (each 8 = 1 skip)
  • 9 — Reverse direction
  • 10 — Burn pile · play again
  • 4 of a kind — Burn pile · play again
